The social network

The Social Network is a biographical drama film released in 2010. It was directed by David Fincher and written by Aaron Sorkin. The movie tells the story of the creation of Facebook and focuses on Mark Zuckerberg, played by Jesse Eisenberg. It shows how Zuckerberg developed the social networking site while studying at Harvard University and the legal problems he faced from former friends and business partners. The film explores themes of ambition, friendship, betrayal, and success. It was critically acclaimed and won three Academy Awards.
