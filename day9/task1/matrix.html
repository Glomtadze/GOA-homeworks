Matrix

The Matrix is a science fiction action film released in 1999. It was directed by The Wachowskis and stars Keanu Reeves as Neo. The story follows Neo, a computer hacker who discovers that the world he lives in is actually a simulated reality created by intelligent machines. With the help of rebels, he fights against the machines to free humanity. The movie is famous for its groundbreaking visual effects, especially the “bullet time” action scenes. It explores themes of reality, freedom, technology, and choice, and became one of the most influential science fiction films of all time.
