inception

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan and released in 2010. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people’s minds by entering their dreams. In the movie, Cobb is given a special task called “inception,” which means planting an idea into someone’s mind instead of stealing one. The story explores themes of dreams, reality, time, and memory. The film is famous for its unique concept, amazing visual effects, and complex storyline. It was a major box office success and won four Academy Awards.
